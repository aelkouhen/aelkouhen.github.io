PUSH Chest · Shoulders · Triceps
Day 1

Push A

Chest emphasis
CHEST
  1. Barbell Bench Press
    4 × 5-6 · 3 min · RPE 8
    Chest · Front delts · Triceps
    Pause at chest. Drive through mid-foot, slight arch, retract scapulae.
  2. Incline DB Press
    3 × 8-10 · 2 min
    Upper chest · Front delts · Triceps
    30° bench. Dumbbells stack over elbows at the bottom, no flare.
SHOULDERS
  1. Seated DB Shoulder Press
    3 × 8-10 · 2 min
    Front delts · Side delts · Triceps
    Back supported. Press in a slight arc, lock out without shrugging.
  2. Cable Lateral Raise
    4 × 12-15 · 60 sec
    Side delts
    Cable behind body. Lead with elbow, slow eccentric, no momentum.
TRICEPS
  1. Weighted Dips
    3 × 8-12 · 2 min
    Lower chest · Triceps · Front delts
    Slight forward lean for chest emphasis, upright for triceps. Full ROM.
  2. Tricep Pushdown (Cable)
    3 × 10-12 · 60 sec
    Triceps (lateral + medial heads)
    Elbows pinned to ribs. Lock out fully, squeeze 1 sec.
  3. Overhead Rope Triceps Ext.
    3 × 12-15 · 60 sec
    Triceps (long head)
    Arms by ears, full stretch at bottom. Hits the long head.
Day 4

Push B

Shoulder emphasis
CHEST
  1. Flat DB Press
    3 × 8-10 · 2 min
    Chest · Front delts · Triceps
    Greater ROM than barbell. Squeeze chest at top, don&#x27;t clank dumbbells.
  2. Cable Crossover (Low to High)
    3 × 10-12 · 90 sec
    Upper chest · Front delts
    Pulleys low, hands meet above eye line. Upper chest fiber bias.
  3. Machine Chest Press
    3 × 10-12 · 90 sec
    Chest · Front delts · Triceps
    Fixed path, brutal stretch. Squeeze pecs at lockout, controlled negative.
  4. Flat Bench Cable Flyes
    3 × 12-15 · 60 sec
    Chest (inner + outer fibers)
    Slight elbow bend, hug a tree. Constant cable tension across full ROM.
SHOULDERS
  1. Standing Overhead Press
    4 × 5-6 · 3 min · RPE 8
    Delts · Triceps · Traps
    Strict, no leg drive. Squeeze glutes, brace abs. Bar over mid-foot.
  2. DB Lateral Raise
    4 × 10-12 · 60 sec
    Side delts
    Slight forward lean. Lead with elbow. Stop just above parallel.
REAR DELTS
  1. Reverse Pec Deck
    3 × 12-15 · 60 sec
    Rear delts
    Lead with pinkies. Squeeze rear delts, don&#x27;t shrug.
TRICEPS
  1. Close-Grip Bench Press
    3 × 8-10 · 2 min
    Triceps · Inner chest · Front delts
    Grip just inside shoulder width. Elbows tucked. Triceps + inner chest.
  2. Skull Crushers (EZ-Bar)
    3 × 8-10 · 90 sec
    Triceps (all heads)
    Lower to forehead or behind head. Keep elbows tucked, don&#x27;t flare.
PULL Back · Rear delts · Biceps
Day 2

Pull A

Back width
BACK
  1. Weighted Pull-Up
    4 × 5-6 · 3 min
    Lats · Mid-back · Biceps
    Full hang to chin over bar. Add weight when you hit 6 clean reps.
  2. Barbell Row (Pendlay/Bent-over)
    3 × 6-8 · 2.5 min
    Lats · Mid-back · Rear delts · Biceps
    Torso ~45°. Pull bar to lower ribs. Strict, no body English.
  3. Chest-Supported Row
    3 × 10-12 · 2 min
    Mid-back · Lats · Rear delts
    Squeeze 1 sec at peak contraction. Eliminates lower-back assistance.
  4. Lat Pulldown (Neutral Grip)
    3 × 10-12 · 90 sec
    Lats · Biceps
    Lean back ~15°. Drive elbows down and back, full stretch top.
REAR DELTS
  1. Face Pull (Cable, Rope)
    4 × 12-15 · 60 sec
    Rear delts · Traps · Mid-back
    Pull to nose, external rotation, elbows high. Shoulder health gold.
BICEPS
  1. Barbell / EZ-Bar Curl
    3 × 8-10 · 90 sec
    Biceps (both heads)
    Elbows pinned at sides. No swing, control the negative.
  2. Incline DB Curl
    3 × 10-12 · 60 sec
    Biceps (long head)
    Bench at 45°. Arms behind torso for maximum long-head stretch.
FOREARMS
  1. Farmer's Walk
    3 rounds · ~30 m
    Forearms · Traps · Core
    Stand tall, shoulders down. Heavy DBs or trap-bar. Grip + traps + core.
Day 5

Pull B

Back thickness
BACK
  1. Conventional / Sumo Deadlift
    3 × 3-5 · 3-4 min · RPE 7-8
    Posterior chain · Lats · Traps · Grip
    Brace, hinge, push the floor away. No grinders. Reset every rep.
  2. T-Bar Row / Seal Row
    3 × 8-10 · 2 min
    Mid-back · Lats · Rear delts
    Pull elbows back and down. Squeeze shoulder blades together at top.
  3. Lat Pulldown (Wide Grip)
    3 × 10-12 · 90 sec
    Lats (upper fibers, width)
    Slightly wider than shoulders. Drive elbows down to ribs.
  4. Single-Arm DB Row
    3 × 10-12 · 90 sec
    Lats · Mid-back · Rear delts
    Pull dumbbell to hip. Slight rotation OK. Full stretch each rep.
  5. Seated Cable Row
    4 × 10-12 · 90 sec
    Mid-back · Lats · Rear delts
    Chest up, drive elbows back. Squeeze 1 sec, slow stretch forward.
TRAPS
  1. Shrugs (Barbell or DB)
    3 × 10-12 · 90 sec
    Upper traps · Grip
    Straight up, not roll. Pause 2 sec at top. Use straps if grip is the limit.
BICEPS
  1. Hammer Curl
    3 × 10-12 · 60 sec
    Brachialis · Biceps · Forearms
    Neutral grip. Hits the brachialis (size driver under biceps).
  2. Cable Curl (Rope)
    3 × 12-15 · 60 sec
    Biceps (peak emphasis)
    Constant tension. Twist wrists out at top for peak contraction.
  3. Preacher Curl
    3 × 10-12 · 60 sec
    Biceps (short head bias)
    Elbows pinned on pad. Don't bounce out of the bottom; full stretch.
FOREARMS
  1. Wrist Curl Superset
    3 × 15 + 15 · 60 sec
    Forearms (flexors + extensors)
    Palms-up wrist curl to failure, flip to palms-down (reverse) for the same reps.
LEGS Quads · Hams · Glutes · Calves · Core
Day 3

Legs A

Quad emphasis
QUADS
  1. Back Squat (High Bar)
    4 × 5-6 · 3-4 min · RPE 8
    Quads · Glutes · Lower back · Core
    Brace hard. Knees track over toes. Hit depth (hip crease below knee).
  2. Bulgarian Split Squat (DB)
    3 × 8/leg · 2 min
    Quads · Glutes
    Rear foot elevated. Front knee tracks forward, torso slightly inclined.
  3. Leg Press
    3 × 10-12 · 2 min
    Quads · Glutes
    Feet lower on platform for quads. Don&#x27;t lock out. Full ROM.
  4. Leg Extension
    3 × 12-15 · 60 sec
    Quads (rectus femoris + vastus)
    Pause 1 sec at top, slow eccentric. Isolates the quads.
HAMSTRINGS
  1. Seated Leg Curl
    3 × 10-12 · 90 sec
    Hamstrings
    Long stretch at top, full contraction at bottom. Better than lying for hams.
CALVES
  1. Standing Calf Raise
    4 × 8-10 · 90 sec
    Calves (gastrocnemius)
    Full ROM: deep stretch at bottom, 1 sec hold at top. Heavy.
CORE
  1. Hanging Leg Raise
    3 × 10-12 · 60 sec
    Abs · Hip flexors
    Posterior tilt at top. No swing. Bent knee version if straight too hard.
  2. Ab Wheel Rollout
    3 × 10-15 · 60 sec
    Abs · Core stabilizers
    Posterior tilt, ribs down. Knees or toes. No lower-back sag at full extension.
Day 6

Legs B

Posterior chain
QUADS
  1. Front Squat / Hack Squat
    3 × 8-10 · 2.5 min
    Quads · Glutes · Core
    Upright torso (front squat) or fixed angle (hack). Quad-dominant.
  2. Walking Lunge (DB)
    3 × 10/leg · 2 min
    Quads · Glutes · Hamstrings
    Long stride for glutes, short stride for quads. Knee just kisses floor.
HAMSTRINGS
  1. Romanian Deadlift
    4 × 6-8 · 3 min
    Hamstrings · Glutes · Lower back
    Hip-hinge, slight knee bend. Bar drags shins. Stretch hams hard.
  2. Lying Leg Curl
    3 × 10-12 · 90 sec
    Hamstrings
    Point toes for ham bias, dorsiflex for calf assist. Slow eccentric.
GLUTES
  1. Barbell Hip Thrust
    3 × 8-10 · 2 min
    Glutes · Hamstrings
    Upper back on bench, chin tucked. Squeeze glutes hard, 2 sec hold top.
CALVES
  1. Seated Calf Raise
    4 × 12-15 · 60 sec
    Calves (soleus)
    Knees bent isolates soleus. Pause at top, full stretch at bottom.
CORE
  1. Cable Woodchopper
    3 × 12/side · 60 sec
    Obliques · Core
    Hips stay square. Anti-rotation focus. Pivot through the obliques.
CONDITIONING
  1. Sled Push (Conditioning)
    3 rounds · ~20 m
    Quads · Glutes · Calves · Conditioning
    Low angle, drive through the balls of the feet. Heavy enough to fatigue, not crawl.

Progression Model

Compound Lifts

When you hit the top of the rep range on all sets:

  • +2.5 kg upper body lifts
  • +5 kg lower body lifts
Example: Bench 4 × 4-6
Week 180 kg × 4,4,4,4
Week 280 kg × 5,5,4,4
Week 380 kg × 6,6,5,5
Week 480 kg × 6,6,6,6
Week 582.5 × 4,4,4,4

Isolation: add weight once you reach top rep target with clean form.

Cardio

  • 2-3 sessions per week
  • 20-30 min per session
  • Incline walking, cycling, rowing, or zone-2
  • Perform after weights or on Sunday
  • Skip if it impairs recovery from lifting

Recovery Rules

  • Sleep: 7.5 to 9 h, consistent timing
  • Protein: 1.6 to 2.2 g/kg body weight daily
  • Hydration: 3 to 4 L/day
  • Creatine: 5 g/day, any time
  • Deload every 8 to 10 weeks (reduce volume ~50%)
  • Log every set: weight, reps, RPE

Warm-Up · 8 min

  • 3 min bike or row easy
  • Mobility: hips, T-spine, shoulders
  • Activation: band pull-aparts, scap pulls
  • Ramp sets: 50% × 5, 70% × 3, 85% × 1
  • Add 1 specific warm-up per heavy compound

Key Tips

  • Focus on progressive overload session by session
  • Control the tempo, full range of motion
  • Train with intensity, not ego
  • Nutrition + sleep drive the results
  • Stay consistent + patient across months, not weeks
